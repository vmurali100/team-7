    Puthalapattu is an assembly constituency in Andhra Pradesh. In 2019, this constituency was won by the Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party. Puthalapattu under Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh State. In 2019, M. Babu of Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party won the seat by defeating Lalitha Kumari from Telugu Desam Party with a margin of 29163 votes.
