    Avanigadda is an assembly constituency in Andhra Pradesh. In 2019, this constituency was won by the Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party. Avanigadda under Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh State. In 2019, Simhadri Ramesh Babu of the Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party won the seat by defeating Mandali Buddha Prasad of the Telugu Desam Party with a margin of 20725 votes. Avanigadda Assembly constituency falls under the Machilipatnam Lok sabha constituency. In the 2019 Lok Sabha elections, Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party candidate Balashowry Vallabhaneni won from Machilipatnam Lok Sabha (MP) Seat with the margin of 60141 votes by defeating Konakalla Narayana Rao of the Telugu Desam Party.
